Welcome to the Janua Waldorf School

Education from the Inside Out.

Since 1981, the Janua Waldorf School has provided a proven alternative in education for children from preschool, kindergarten to Grade 8.  The Waldorf approach works from the inside out, focusing on giving children a deep confidence in their own learning process, and having a deep sense of wonder and respect for life and learning. Learn more about why so many parents like you, choose Waldorf education.

We’ve built over 35 years of experience educating young minds from the earliest ages. Our early years programs are comfortable and home-like, providing a safe and happy learning environment for Pre-K children, while our Lower and Middle School programs are focused on each stage of a child’s development in these important years.

Come and discover why JANUA could just be the perfect alternative for your child.
